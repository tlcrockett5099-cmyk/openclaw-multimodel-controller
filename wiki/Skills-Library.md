# Skills Library

> AI Skills and Gemini Gems in Openclaw MultiModel Controller

---

## What Are Skills?

**Skills** are pre-configured system prompts that give the AI a specific persona or specialization. When you activate a skill, it's automatically injected into every conversation as context, shaping how the AI responds.

Examples:
- Activate "Code Reviewer" → AI analyzes your code with security and best-practice focus
- Activate "Email Composer" → AI writes polished, professional emails
- Activate "Tutor" → AI explains concepts step-by-step for learning

---

## Finding and Activating Skills

1. Open the **Skills** tab (sparkles ✨ icon in navigation)
2. Browse by category or search by name/description
3. Click **Activate** on any skill card
4. The skill is now active — a banner appears in the chat confirming this

You can activate multiple skills at once. All active skills are combined and sent as context to the AI.

## Creating Custom Skills

1. Scroll to the bottom of the Skills page
2. Click **+ Add Custom Skill**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name** — e.g., "Legal Drafter"
   - **Category** — choose from the list
   - **Icon** — an emoji
   - **Description** — what this skill does
   - **System Prompt** — the instruction sent to the AI
4. Click **Save**

Your custom skill appears in the list and can be activated like built-in skills.

## Tips for Using Skills

- **Combine skills**: Stack a "Research Assistant" with "Technical Writer" for documented reports
- **Skill specificity**: More specific system prompts give better results
- **Per-conversation**: Active skills apply to all new conversations — deactivate when done
- **Gemini Gems**: For best results with Gems, use with Gemini 2.0 Flash or Gemini 2.5 Pro
