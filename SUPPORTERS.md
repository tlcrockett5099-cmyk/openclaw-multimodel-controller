# OpenClaw Supporters ❤

A heartfelt thank you to everyone who has supported OpenClaw on Patreon.

OpenClaw is built and maintained by one developer in their spare time.
Every donation — no matter the size — directly funds new features,
bug fixes, and keeping the project alive.

## How to get your name listed

1. Donate $5+/month at [patreon.com/TLG3D](https://patreon.com/TLG3D)
2. Open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/SerThrocken/openclaw-multimodel-controller/issues/new)
   with the title "Add me to SUPPORTERS" and your preferred display name.
3. Your name will be added in the next release.
